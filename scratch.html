Coorg , Karnatka

Surrounded by imposing mountains and covered by mist,Coorg is a nture's lover's paradise.This small town in Karnatka is best known for it's diverse flora and fauna and aromatic spicies and coffee plantations. from waterfalls and trekking routes to forts and temples.

Oaty and Coonor in Tamil Nadu

Ooty , the queen of Hill Station's balances the hustle and bustle of the life with expansive tea gardens.it's famous miniature toy train is listed in the world heritage site.Covering a distance of 19 km , the rail route offers reviatalizing travel experience.

Chennai , Tamil Nadu

Chennai , the capital of Tamil Nadu,is the blend of the old and the new.It is often discribed as the keeper of south Indian traditions. While on one hand it flaunts spectacular architecture from the city's past, on the other, it is known for it's cosmopliton lifestyle.With something to offer everyone, Chennai is one of the most visited family destination.

Hampi , Karnatka

Hampi is one of the top places to visit in India.The World Heritage site take visistors back in time to the 15th and 16th century with it's magnificant ruins.It makes everyone's imagination come alive with remnants of temples,monuments and havelis. Quaint cafes set up on rooftops offers a variety of cuisines.

Mahablipuram, Tamil Nadu

Mahablipuram , once a major sea port,is now a must visit World Heritage site. From carved relief work in stones to cave temples, Mahabalipuram overflows with architectural wonders.Sunsets are extra special here as the soft glow of setting sun adds to the allure of these monuments and there is a mystery of Krishana's butterbull. The city's proximity to Chennai makes it one of the popular long weekend in South India.
